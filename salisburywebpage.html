About Me

As a dedicated Project Director at Central Wyoming College, I specialize in student success initiatives. My work primarily focuses on the TRIO Talent Search program, where I lead efforts to identify and assist individuals from disadvantaged backgrounds who have the potential to succeed in higher education.

My professional philosophy centers on removing barriers to education. Whether it is providing academic counseling, financial guidance, or career planning, I am committed to empowering students in the Riverton community and beyond.

Community Leadership: In addition to my academic role, I have served as a Riverton City Council Member, advocating for local development and civic engagement.

Institutional Focus: Central Wyoming College

Central Wyoming College (CWC) is a designated Native American Serving Non-Tribal Institution (NASNTI) with a mission deeply rooted in community empowerment. My work aligns with the college's core values:

  • Mission: "Succeeding Together Against All Odds." We focus on serving students from Fremont, Hot Springs, and Teton counties.
  • TRIO Programs: As part of the Student Affairs division, our department plays a critical role in supporting first-generation and low-income students through programs like Talent Search, Upward Bound, and Student Support Services.
  • Community Impact: CWC acts as a cultural and educational hub in Riverton, bridging the gap between secondary education and workforce development.
